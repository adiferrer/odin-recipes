Korean Corn Cheese Ramen Recipe

This dish levels up the ordinary instant ramen. Best suitable for a snack or light meal. Good for 1 person. Takes up 10-15 minutes preparation and cooking time.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 pack of any instant ramen noodles
  • 1 cup of assorted cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup of spring onion

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles and set aside soup base sachet
  2. Strain noodles
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat
  4. Saute corn, and add the sugar and mayonnaise
  5. Add the strained noodles and soup base
  6. Cook, stirring occasionally to mix well
  7. Add cheese and cover lid for 1 minute
  8. Top with the spring onions
  9. Ready to eat!
